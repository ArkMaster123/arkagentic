Why I Built This

I was tired of boring virtual meetings. Zoom calls, Google Meet, Teams... they all feel the same. Staring at a grid of faces, waiting for your turn to unmute. It's exhausting.

I wanted a cool place to hang out with online friends that feels more like being together, even when we're not physically in the same room.

So I built ArkAgentic - a virtual world where you can walk around, bump into AI agents, and video chat with friends when you're in the same room. It's like the metaverse, but actually fun and useful.

The AI agents aren't just chatbots. They're specialists you can consult - a research agent, a strategy analyst, a writing editor, and more. Walk up to them, have a conversation, get real help.
